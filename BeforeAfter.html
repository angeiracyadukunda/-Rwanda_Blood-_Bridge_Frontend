What to Do Before, During, and After Blood Donation

Proper preparation and care can make your blood donation experience smooth and positive. Follow these tips to ensure you are ready before, comfortable during, and well after your donation.

Before Donation

  • Eat a healthy meal before you donate. Avoid fatty foods.
  • Drink plenty of water before your appointment.
  • Get a good night's sleep the night before.
  • Avoid alcohol and caffeine on the day of donation.
  • Wear a shirt with sleeves that can be rolled up.
  • Bring your ID and any donor card if you have one.

During Donation

  • Relax and take deep breaths. Stay calm.
  • Inform the staff if you feel uncomfortable or unwell.
  • Squeeze the stress ball provided to help the blood flow.
  • Enjoy the snacks and refreshments offered after donation.

After Donation

  • Drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated.
  • Avoid strenuous activities for at least 24 hours.
  • Keep the bandage on for a few hours and avoid using that arm heavily.
  • Eat a healthy meal with iron-rich foods to replenish your body.
  • If you feel lightheaded or dizzy, sit or lie down until you feel better.
  • Remember, you can donate again in 8 weeks (56 days).

Ready to Donate?

Follow these tips to ensure a successful donation experience. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to contact us.
